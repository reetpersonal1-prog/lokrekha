About Lokrekha

LOKREKHA is a celebration of India’s rich artistic heritage. Rooted in the traditional Sohrai and Madhubani painting styles, each piece reflects culture, storytelling, and craftsmanship passed through generations. Our mission is to bring these indigenous art forms to contemporary fashion while preserving their authenticity.
